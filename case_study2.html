Senseware is an IoT PaaS (Platform as a service) startup that wirelessly connects people to their buildings' data- saving them time, money, and energy.

I was hired last May as their Visual Design Contract Lead. I have worked remotely for them from school in Clemson. Since coming onboard I have been given visual ownership of everything from marketing case studies to a rebranding overhaul.

It has been a learning experience for me and for Senseware, learning how to find a company voice and identity. Over the last 8 months I have been able to create cleaner visuals and overall visual continuity across the different platforms of Senseware. This was achieved by creating brand assets with a uniform icon and spot illustration style, bringing in additional typefaces, broadening their color palette, and refreshing their old material.

I have worked on landing pages, infographics, case studies, ebooks, email campaigns, and internal and external decks. Many of my icons and illustrations appear on Senseware.co.
